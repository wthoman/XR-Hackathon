# Testing Your Lens On Spectacles

You can install and upload Lenses to your Spectacles devices directly from Lens Studio by selecting `Preview Lens` in the toolbar or `Send to Spectacles` in the Additional Settings popup.

Ensure the project is built for Spectacles by navigating to Project Info Settings → Lens Made For Spectacles before using these options.

Lenses sent through Lens Studio will appear in the Lens Explorer \- Draft Section. Draft Lenses are local to your device and not visible to others, intended for experimentation or testing before publishing.

</p>

## Send to Connected Spectacles

Clicking `Preview Lens` on the toolbar or `Send To Spectacles` in the Additional Settings popup will install the Lens on the device currently awake and connected to Lens Studio. For instructions on connecting your Spectacles to Lens Studio, refer to the [Connecting Lens Studio to Spectacles](./connecting-lens-studio-to-spectacles.mdx) documentation.
