# Hackathon Showcase

We're thrilled to showcase the incredible work from our vibrant community of developers. These innovative projects demonstrate the limitless possibilities of Spectacles and highlight the creativity, talent, and vision of our growing ecosystem. Across the globe, talented developers are pushing the boundaries of what's possible with Spectacles. From transformative healthcare applications to immersive entertainment experiences, our hackathon participants continue to inspire us with their creativity.
